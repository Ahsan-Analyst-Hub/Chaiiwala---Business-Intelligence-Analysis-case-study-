## 🟤 Project Title

**Chaiiwala -- Customer Experience Business Intelligence Platform**

### Introduction

Chaiiwala is a UK-based Indian/Pakistani street food café chain (Coffee Shop), established in Leicester in 2015. Known for its signature Karak Chaii, it serves authentic Indian drinks, 
street food, and fusion meals in a relaxed, community-focused atmosphere. The brand has expanded to over 140 locations worldwide and have 114 stores in UK alone.

---

## 🎯 Project Overview

This project is a full end-to-end **Coffee Shop Customer Experience Analytics solution** built using:

* SQL Server (Data Warehouse)
* Power Query (ETL & Data Cleaning)
* Power BI (Data Modelling & Dashboarding)
* DAX (Advanced Business Logic)

It simulates a real-world retail analytics system designed to measure and improve customer experience across multiple store locations in UK.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem

Retail leadership lacked visibility into:

* Customer experience across stores in UK
* Service quality variation by region
* Operational issues (stock, seating, cleanliness)
* Relationship between customer experience and revenue
* Store-level performance accountability

---

## 🎯 Objective

To design a BI (Business Intelligence) system that answers:

* Which stores provide the best/worst customer experience?
* What operational factors impact performance?
* Does CXI (Customer Experience Index) impact revenue?
* Which regions require intervention?
* What actions should management take?

## 📊 Key KPIs Developed

* Customer Experience Index (CXI)
* Store Rank (CXI & Revenue)
* Revenue at Risk
* Customer Satisfaction Score
* Data Trust Score
* Promotion Awareness Score
* Skip & Timeout Rate

---

## 📈 Dashboard Pages

1. Executive Overview
2. Regional Intelligence
3. Store Performance
4. Root Cause Analysis
5. Revenue vs Customer Experience
6. Customer Behaviour
7. Executive Action Centre

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Customer experience varies significantly across stores
* Stock availability is the strongest driver of poor CXI
* Revenue is positively correlated with CXI
* A small number of stores drive majority of operational issues
* Actionable insights enable targeted intervention
